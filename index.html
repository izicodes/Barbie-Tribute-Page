Barbie

Barbie is a fashion doll manufactured by the American toy company Mattel, Inc. and launched in March 1959. American businesswoman Ruth Handler is credited with the creation of the doll using a German doll called Bild Lilli as her inspiration.

She is a fashion icon and have a large wardrobe filled with designer clothing and accessories. She would be well-traveled, having visited many exotic locations and participating in various adventurous activities. She would have a large social circle, including many friends and admirers.

As a fictional character, Barbie has had many different careers over the years including a nurse, astronaut, teacher, and even a presidential candidate. If she were a real person, she might be ambitious and determined, with a variety of interests and talents.
